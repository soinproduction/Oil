Cookies

Cookies are small text files designed to track the behavior of page visitors. Our service and web pages use cookies to keep you updated. In order to improve the service, visitors must allow the use of cookies.Cookies allow the page owner to see how many visitors are on the page, what visitors are doing on the page and where the visitor came from the page. (Google, URL or others)To the extent required by applicable law, we will obtain your consent before placing minor cookies or similar technologies on your computer. You can prevent certain types of cookies from being downloaded to your computer by selecting the appropriate settings in your web browser. Most web browsers tell you how to stop accepting new browser cookies, how to be notified when a new browser cookie is received, and how to disable existing cookies. You can find out how to do this for your specific browser by clicking Help in the browser menu or by visiting www.allaboutcookies.org. The site is not intended to respond to "do not track" signals received from browsers. Please note that without cookies or other automated tools, you will not be able to use all the functions of the Site.We do not collect personally identifiable information about your online activities over time or through third party websites, devices, or other online services. On the Site, we use third-party online analytics services such as Google Analytics. To the extent required by applicable law, we will obtain your consent before using third-party analytics cookies. The service providers who administer these analytics services use automated technologies to collect data (such as email addresses, IP addresses, cookies and other device identifiers), for example, to evaluate the use of the Site and diagnose technical problems. To learn more about Google Analytics and how to manage your preferences, please visit: www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html.
